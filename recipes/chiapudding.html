Rosewater and Coconut Chia Pudding

Ingredients

  • Chia seeds
  • Coconut milk
  • Rose water
  • Desiccated coconut

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Leave mixture to rest for twenty minutes, mixing every now and then.
  3. Put in the fridge, letting it rest a few hours or overnight. The longer the better!

Buckwheat Bowls with Sprouts and Tahini Sauce

Ingredients

For the sauce

  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Maple syrup
  • Garlic, minced
  • Cold water

For the bowls

  • Buckwheat (grains)
  • Sprouts, halved
  • Chickpeas (canned)
  • Ras el Hanout
  • Olive oil

Instructions

Sauce

  1. Mix the ingredients for the sauce. Set aside.
  2. Boil the buckwheat according to the instructions on the package.

Crispy spiced chickpeas

  1. Drain the chickpeas and dry them well in a clean tea towel.
  2. Mix the spices with the oil.
  3. Put the chickpeas in a medium bowl and drizzle with the oil and spice mixture. Toss to coat.
  4. Bake at 200C for about 40 minutes, until golden and crispy. Set aside.

Roasted sprouts

  1. Toss halved sprouts with some oil to coat them.
  2. Put them on a baking tin lined with parchment paper.
  3. Roast in the over (200C) for 15-20 minutes, flipping the sprouts halfway.

Assembling the bowls

  1. Boil the buckwheat following the instructions on the package.
  2. Put a large spoonful of buckwheat at the bottom of each bowl.
  3. Add the toppings and pour the sauce over the contents of the bowl.

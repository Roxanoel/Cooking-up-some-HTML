Roxane's Gluten-Free Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Gluten-Free Casarecce Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Vintage Red Leicester, grated
  • Minced garlic

Preparation

  1. Boil the pasta according to instructions on the pack.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan.
  3. Add garlic and cook at low heat for about a minute.
  4. Add the flour and form a roux. Let it cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add the milk little by little, whisking often to avoid lumps.
  6. Add the smoked paprika and stir well.
  7. Add the shredded cheese and stir until creamy and smooth.
  8. Tip the drained pasta into the sauce and stir well. Serve immediately.
